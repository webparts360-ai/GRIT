GRIT: Larico Team Playbook

Creating a digital workplace to enhance work culture

The Principle: The Agency Gap

Traditional Performance Management (OKRs) often fails because outcomes are a mix of market forces and leadership strategy—factors employees cannot control. We focus on Execution: the daily work culture and effort that is 100% actionable.

Market & Strategy

Determined by leaders and economy. Low employee agency.

Execution (Work Culture)

Actionable, measurable, and entirely within team control.

🤝

Generosity

Sharing info early. Engineers sharing rough foundation loads with procurement to kill lead-time lag.

🌀

Resilience

Mastering the pivot. Speed of reorganization when tower locations are blocked on site.

🛡️

Integrity

Reporting the truth. Admitting technical near-misses immediately to fix systems before accidents occur.

💎

Trust

Information symmetry. Foremen and CEOs looking at the same live, unsanitized data dashboard.

Culture vs. Traditional Metrics

Execution is driven by social intelligence and interdependence. We measure how well people connect, not just how hard they work alone.

Site Safety & Social IQ

The Social Contract

Execution is a social act. We don't keep score on hours; we keep score on how well we protect the tribe and the project timeline. Larico is an organism, not just a contract.
